CUBE

CUBE contract: Etherscan

Basics

Tokens are created upon buy and destroyed upon sell.
Token price rises and falls with token supply.
20% of ETH from buys/sells is distributed to CUBE holders.
On buy: 1/3 of the 20% is given to the referrer (if provided).

CUBE is a modified version of PoWH3D: Etherscan

Modifications:

- Connected to FomoCube username contract
- Removed investment requirement for referrals
- Removed token transfer fee
- Added ability to deposit ETH to CUBE holders as dividends
- Replaced Ambassador system

POLY

POLY contract: Etherscan

Basics

POLY has the same price formula as Fomo3D.
(This means it starts low and rises slowly)

POLY tokens last for one round and cannot be sold.
On buy: 10% is given to the referrer (if provided) or CUBE holders.
The jackpot is awarded to the leader when the timer runs out.

POLY is evolution of Fomo3D Long:

The Increment
